What you’ll find here

We curate living documentation from developers, designers, researchers, and product leaders building at the frontier of AI. Expect a blend of architecture breakdowns, experiments gone right (and wrong), and teachable artifacts you can remix.

  • Code-forward storytelling

    Every article pairs concepts with runnable snippets, repos, or notebooks so you can learn by doing.

  • Real-world retrospectives

    We share the systems thinking behind launches, including mistakes, mitigations, and iteration loops.

  • An open builder community

    Guest contributors bring perspectives from startups, research labs, and indie hacking—everyone’s invited.

Our publishing values

  • Clarity over hype

    We unpack complex systems without buzzwords so teams can make better decisions, faster.

  • Evidence-driven

    Every insight is anchored in shipped product work, measurable experiments, or reproducible research.

  • Community first

    We spotlight people behind the breakthroughs, celebrate collaboration, and encourage open knowledge.
